Privacy Policy for Lawyer's Diary

Effective Date: 18/07/2026

This privacy policy strictly governs the use of the "Lawyer's Diary" application created for Android devices.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Lawyer's Diary is designed as a 100% offline application. We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or store your personal information, client details, or case data on any external servers. All information entered into the application is saved locally on your device's internal storage.

2. App Permissions

To function properly, the app requests the following local device permissions:

  • Camera & Storage Access: Required solely for attaching documents or cause list images to your daily tasks.
  • Notifications: Required to send you local time-based reminders for your scheduled case hearings and meetings.

3. Third-Party Services

Because this app operates entirely offline, it does not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services that harvest user data.

4. Data Security

Since all data remains on your physical device, the security of your information is intrinsically tied to your device's security. We recommend using standard device locks (PIN, Pattern, Biometrics) to protect your sensitive legal data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the app.
